Our Approach to Malta

Seagull Maritime Ltd is a Maltese company, registered in Malta, operating under a group of international management system standards. This section sets out how we have chosen to engage with the Licensing Board — openly, proactively, and on the front foot.

Stance
This is a voluntary compliance pack. We are not waiting for a request — we are making everything the Schedule asks for available, in one organised place, so that a reviewer can see the state of our system at a glance. If the Board wishes to issue interim recognition, we are ready. If the Board wishes to conduct a formal review, we are ready.

Why this portal exists. Seagull Maritime has operated in Malta since 2011 (company registration C/54665/2011) and has built a group-wide management system that speaks directly to the requirements of S.L. 480.05 — the Licensing of Private Maritime Security Companies Regulations. Rather than wait for a licensing question, we have collated the evidence the Licensing Board would expect to see and organised it against the Schedule to those Regulations.

What this is not. This is not a formal application. It is not a legal submission. It is a structured, confidential briefing pack — prepared on our own initiative — that allows any authorised reviewer to walk through the evidence we hold, in the order the regulations set out.

How we operate in practice. Seagull deploys PCASP through a combination of Maltese corporate structure, internationally-recognised accreditation, and region-specific operating procedures. Every armed deployment is governed by a group Rules on the Use of Force procedure, supported by pre-deployment risk assessment, client vetting, and personnel competence verification.

Our cooperation posture. We welcome scrutiny. We answer questions promptly. Where a reviewer identifies something they would like to see strengthened or clarified, our default answer is yes. The management system on show in this portal is reviewed continuously — and a live feed of updates is maintained in the Live section.

How to use this portal

Six sections, each mapped to an element of the S.L. 480.05 Schedule. Every card on the homepage opens a dedicated page. Where a document itself is referenced, it can be requested directly — contact details below.

Navigation. Return to the homepage at any time to see the full chapter map. Each section is self-contained and can be reviewed in any order.

Live updates. The Live section carries a rolling record of document changes and new releases — so a returning reviewer can see at a glance what has moved since last visit.

Document requests. Individual policies, procedures, registers and personnel records are available on request. Please contact Darren Watts, Group Compliance Director (darren@seagullsecurity.com) with the document reference or description.
